As a mom to a curious 6-year-old daughter, Rashmi noticed her little one's endless questions about Indian festivals, each one a bridge to their roots. It hit her then: our kids shouldn't just hear tales, they should dance with the gods, soar with the heroes, and carry them like treasures. Her daughter's awe for Hanuman, her caped crusader, birthed Chotu and Chutki - spirited characters blending mythic valor with modern flair.
