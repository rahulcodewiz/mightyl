About Rashmi

Discover the many facets of the creator behind Mighty Loka

Rashmi Mardur

Founder & Creative Director

Rashmi is an IT professional, unraveling puzzles and sparking innovation, but her soul sings through storytelling, sharing India's timeless heritage with young dreamers. Through Mighty Loka, Rashmi crafts adventures that weave Hindu wisdom into tales young hearts can't resist. She envisions a world where these stories live anew, as thrilling as any superhero saga.

As the founder of Mighty Loka, Rashmi oversees the entire creative process from concept to publication. Her vision is to create high-quality children's books that not only entertain but also educate young minds about Indian culture and values.

As a mom to a curious 6-year-old daughter, Rashmi noticed her little one's endless questions about Indian festivals, each one a bridge to their roots. It hit her then: our kids shouldn't just hear tales, they should dance with the gods, soar with the heroes, and carry them like treasures. Her daughter's awe for Hanuman, her caped crusader, birthed Chotu and Chutki - spirited characters blending mythic valor with modern flair.
